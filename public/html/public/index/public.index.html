The Studio

Made You Look is Toronto’s most established professional collective jewellery studio. We act as an umbrella company that supports and promotes our 20 self-employed resident designers. These highly skilled designers create their work on-site in our fully equipped manufacturing studio that is open 24 hours a day 7 days a week.

Our Handmade Jewellery

Our Queen West Store showcases the work of over 100 local Toronto Jewellery Designers. Our offerings feature a wide range of materials and price points, from exquisite high end work to fun and funky “must have” accessories. Here is just a sampling to whet your appetite!

Custom Design

Creating unique customized jewellery is our specialty! Do you have something in mind that you are having trouble finding? Meet with one of our talented in-house designers to design, develop and create a one-of-a-kind piece of jewellery that is just right for you!

Wedding and Engagement

In our downtown store and on-line we feature an amazing selection of beautifully made wedding and engagement rings. Organized by designer, each artist offers their own unique approach to designing and creating these classic symbols of the love continuum.
